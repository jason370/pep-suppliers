Help Center

Are products for research use only? +
Yes. All products are intended for laboratory research use only. Not for human consumption, diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of disease.
What's the difference between domestic and overseas? +
Domestic: 2-5 days, fast shipping, higher prices. Overseas: 2-3 weeks, significant savings (50-70% below market), direct lab sourcing.
Do you provide COAs or third-party testing? +
Most products include available supplier documentation. Many have COAs or Janoshik testing available. Optional upgraded testing may be available on select products.
Can upgraded testing affect shipping time? +
Yes. Optional upgraded testing may affect fulfillment or shipping time on select products.
How do I track my order? +
Use the Order Tracking section. Enter your order number and email address to check status and estimated delivery.
Can I order in bulk? +
Yes. Contact support@pep-suppliers.com for bulk pricing, planned supply schedules, and custom sourcing arrangements.
Why does overseas take longer? +
International sourcing and shipping naturally take longer but offer significant cost savings. Typical 2-3 week delivery depending on product and destination.
Can I mix domestic and overseas items? +
Yes. Orders can include both domestic and overseas items. Fulfillment will follow the longest timeline in the order.
