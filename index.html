FAQ

Clear answers before you choose.

Are your products for research use only?

Yes. Products are intended for laboratory research use only and are not for human consumption, diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of disease.

What is the difference between domestic and overseas?

Domestic options usually ship faster from U.S.-based inventory at a higher price. Overseas options usually offer deeper savings with longer lead times.

Do you provide COAs or third-party testing?

Most products include available documentation, and many products have COAs or Janoshik testing available. Documentation varies by supplier, batch, and sourcing path, so we clearly show what is available before purchase whenever possible.

Can optional testing affect shipping time?

Yes. Optional upgraded testing may be available on select products, but it can add time to fulfillment or shipping.

Can I order in bulk?

Bulk-friendly sourcing may be available. Planned orders typically make it easier to compare price, timeline, and documentation options.
