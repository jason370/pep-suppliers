Frequently Asked Questions

Research-use peptide sourcing

Are products for research use only? ▼

Yes. All products are intended for laboratory research use only. They are not intended for human consumption, diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of disease.

What is the difference between domestic and overseas? ▼

Domestic sources use U.S. warehouses with overnight options and premium pricing. Overseas sources direct from global suppliers with longer 2–3 week shipping and significant savings.

Do you provide COAs or third-party testing? ▼

Many products include available documentation, and many products have COAs or Janoshik testing available. Documentation varies by supplier, batch, and sourcing path. Optional upgraded testing may be available on select products.

Can I order in bulk? ▼

Yes. Bulk orders are encouraged, especially for overseas sourcing where you benefit from discount tiers. Contact support@pep-suppliers.com for bulk pricing and custom orders.
