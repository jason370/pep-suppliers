Frequently Asked Questions

Are your products for research use only?▼

Yes. All products are intended for laboratory research use only. They are not intended for human consumption, diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of disease.

What is the difference between domestic and overseas?▼

Domestic sources use U.S. warehouses for fast shipping with overnight available on select items at premium pricing. Overseas sources direct from global suppliers with longer 2–3 week shipping but significantly lower pricing.

Do you provide COAs or third-party testing?▼

Most products include available documentation, and many products have COAs or Janoshik testing available. Documentation varies by supplier, batch, and sourcing path. Optional upgraded testing may be available on select products, but it can add time to fulfillment or shipping.

Can optional testing affect shipping time?▼

Yes. Optional upgraded testing is available on select products as a premium transparency feature, but it may extend fulfillment or shipping time. Specific timelines will be shown during the order review process.

Can I order in bulk?▼

Yes. Bulk orders are encouraged, especially for overseas sourcing where you benefit from discount tiers. Contact support@pep-suppliers.com for bulk pricing and custom orders.
