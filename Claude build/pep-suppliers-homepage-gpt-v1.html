Frequently Asked Questions

Why are overseas options cheaper? ▼

Overseas options are direct-sourced from manufacturers, avoiding middleman markups. You accept a longer shipping timeline (2–3 weeks) instead of paying for fast logistics and US warehouse inventory. This creates significant cost savings.

Why does overseas shipping take 2–3 weeks? ▼

Overseas sourcing includes synthesis time (5–7 days), international courier shipping (7–10 days), and customs clearance. We provide full tracking throughout. The timeline is predictable but longer than domestic inventory.

Why is domestic more expensive? ▼

Domestic pricing reflects the cost of maintaining US inventory, inventory risk, temperature-controlled storage, and fast dispatch logistics. You pay a premium for convenience and predictable 2–5 day delivery.

Are domestic and overseas products the same quality? ▼

Both sourcing paths use suppliers we've screened. The key difference is sourcing method and timeline, not quality. Documentation availability may vary by supplier, and we're transparent about what's available for each product.

Do you provide Certificates of Analysis (COAs)? ▼

Documentation availability depends on what our suppliers provide. We include available batch documentation with orders. Not all suppliers provide complete COAs, and we're transparent about documentation limitations.

Can I order in bulk? ▼

Yes. Bulk orders are encouraged, especially for overseas sourcing where you benefit from discount tiers. Contact support@pep-suppliers.com for bulk pricing and custom orders.

Can I mix domestic and overseas in one order? ▼

Yes. You can choose domestic (fast, premium) for some products and overseas (slow, discounted) for others in the same order. Select your preference for each item.

Are these products for research use only? ▼

Yes. All products are intended for laboratory research use only. They are not intended for human consumption, diagnosis, treatment, or prevention of disease.
