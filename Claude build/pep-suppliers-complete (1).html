Frequently asked questions

Common questions about domestic vs overseas, pricing, and quality.

Is the overseas option as good quality as domestic? +
Yes. Absolutely. Both options use the same 98%+ purity standard, independent HPLC testing, and full COA documentation. The only difference is timing and pricing. Overseas is fresh synthesis (often higher purity) and cheaper because you can wait.
Why is overseas 70% cheaper? +
Three reasons: (1) Direct from synthesis lab with no warehouse markup, (2) Fresh synthesis made to order (no inventory carrying costs), (3) You accept 2-3 week shipping (no premium for speed). Domestic requires warehouse stock, inventory risk, and fast dispatch logistics.
How long is overseas shipping really? +
Typically 2-3 weeks from order to your door. 5-7 days for synthesis + 7-10 days international courier + customs clearance. We provide full tracking. It's slower but very predictable.
Will customs delay my overseas order? +
Possibly, but rarely. We include full documentation and declarations. "Research purposes only" labeling is standard. Delays are uncommon but possible. If customs is a risk for your timeline, choose domestic instead.
Can I mix domestic and overseas in one order? +
Yes. You can order some peptides from domestic (fast, higher price) and others from overseas (slow, cheaper). Just select your preference for each item at checkout.
What's the catch with overseas pricing? +
No catch. You get lower price in exchange for longer wait time. Quality is identical. Testing is identical. COA is identical. It's simply a different supply chain: warehouse inventory + fast logistics (domestic) vs fresh synthesis + patient shipping (overseas).
Does overseas qualify for bulk discounts? +
Yes. Overseas bulk discounts are even better: 3-5 vials -15%, 6-10 vials -25%, 11+ vials -35%. Domestic doesn't have bulk pricing. That's where overseas really shines for large orders.
Which should I choose? +
Choose domestic if: You need peptides in days (urgent research, pilots). Choose overseas if: You're doing bulk orders, long-term projects, or want max savings. Most researchers do a mix depending on their project timeline.
Is there a money-back guarantee on both? +
Yes. Both domestic and overseas orders come with our full satisfaction guarantee. If a batch fails our 98%+ purity standard or you're unsatisfied, we'll replace or refund you. No questions asked.
How do you handle privacy? +
All orders ship in discreet packaging with no branding or external labeling. We use encrypted communication, HTTPS checkout, and never share customer data with third parties. Your privacy is protected.
What about customs and legal issues? +
All our products are clearly labeled "for research purposes only." Peptide legality varies by jurisdiction. It's your responsibility to verify legality in your location. We ship in compliance with US law and provide all documentation needed for customs clearance.
